About Me

Hello there! My name is Thomas, but online I go as ThomasDev. I'm a high school student from South Africa with a passion for software development and technology.
I discovered my love for programming at around 12 years old and I've been hooked ever since. My first foray into the programming world was with Unity and C#, which I used to create my very first games. None of these games have been completed or published, but this inspired me to continue learning and expand my knowledge as much as possible.

Over time, I expanded my programming skillset to include other languages such as Java, Python, and Delphi. I found that learning these new languages not only helped me to become a better programmer, but it also opened up new opportunities for me to explore different areas of technology.
I've spent countless hours working on personal projects, experimenting with different tools and techniques to create software that is not only functional but also aesthetically pleasing. My passion for programming has also led me to start a blog, where I share my thoughts and experiences with other like-minded individuals.

So that's a bit about me! I hope you enjoyed learning more about my background and interests. Feel free to check out my blog to keep up with my latest projects and musings. Thanks for stopping by!
